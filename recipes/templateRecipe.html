Chicken Curry

By Hetal Vasavada
October 16, 2024

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and ginger; saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in curry powder and salt.
  5. Pour in coconut milk, bring to a simmer, and cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve.

Preparation Time:

30 minutes

Serving Size:

Serves 4

Nutritional Information:

Approximately 450 calories per serving

Tips:

Try adding vegetables like bell peppers or spinach for extra nutrition!
